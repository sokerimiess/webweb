AWARDS

Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope (1977):

Special Achievement Academy Award for Sound Effects (for Ben Burtt).

Received numerous other nominations and wins from various award bodies.

Star Wars: Episode V - The Empire Strikes Back (1980):

Academy Awards: Won 2 Oscars for Best Sound and a

Special Achievement for Visual Effects. It was also nominated

for Best Art Direction and Best Original Score.

Other accolades include wins and nominations from various organizations.

Star Wars: Episode VI - Return of the Jedi (1983):

Academy Awards: Nominated for 4 Oscars including Best Art Direction, Best Sound, Best Sound Effects Editing, and Best Original Score. Received a Special Achievement Award for Visual Effects.

Other accolades include wins and nominations from various organizations.

Other accolades as well.

Star Wars: Episode I - The Phantom Menace (1999):

Academy Awards: Nominated for 3 Oscars including Best Sound, Best Sound Effects Editing, and Best Visual Effects.

Star Wars: Episode II - Attack of the Clones (2002):

Academy Awards: Nominated for Best Visual Effects.

Star Wars: Episode III - Revenge of the Sith (2005):

Academy Awards: Nominated for Best Makeup.

Star Wars: Episode VII - The Force Awakens (2015):

Academy Awards: Nominated for 5 Oscars, including Best Original Score, Best Sound Editing, Best Sound Mixing, Best Film Editing, and Best Visual Effects.

Star Wars: Episode VIII - The Last Jedi (2017):

Academy Awards: Nominated for 4 Oscars, including Best Original Score, Best Sound Editing, Best Sound Mixing, and Best Visual Effects.

Star Wars: Episode IX - The Rise of Skywalker (2019):

Academy Awards: Nominated for 3 Oscars, including Best Original Score, Best Sound Editing, and Best Visual Effects.
